--- STEP 1: Clean up old failed resource group ---
az group delete -n ReverseCodeRG --yes

(wait for it to finish - ~30 seconds)



--- STEP 2: Deploy everything ---
curl -sL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sundhar-04/Reverse-Engineering-Contest/main/deploy-azure.sh | bash

(wait ~3 minutes for VM + Docker + app)




--- IF STEP 2 FAILS WITH REGION ERROR ---
Try with a different region instead:
  centralus
  westeurope
  northeurope
  southeastasia
  westus3

Example:
curl -sL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sundhar-04/Reverse-Engineering-Contest/main/deploy-azure.sh | bash -s -- westeurope

Or check available regions first:
  az vm list-skus --size Standard_B2s --all --output table
